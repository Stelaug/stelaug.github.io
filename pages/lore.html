Anders Tale, a young being filled with hopes and dreams.
Though not of the talkative type, he's always wished others well.
Sadly, Anders quickly learns that those who stand out, will be left out.
Anders, not one to admit defeat so fast, takes matters into his own hands.
He tries time and time again, yet, to no avail.
But after countless attempts, he believes to have found a solution.
Cold, bitter revenge.
Now, blinded by anger and grief, he sees the world in a new light.
Anders is now hellbent on bringing the world to its knees.
He fought for a world that did not welcome him, but now, their time to suffer has come.
With an iron will and determination, he shall ensure the world is devoured by flames.
But soon thereafter, Anders must realize he is not omnipitent.
Lightning splits the heavens and a terrible thunder roars over Scandinavia,
with an elegance like the Northern Lights and as relentless as the bitter cold,
Anders is forced to his knees, his newfound dream shatters.
Without a warning, He has awakened from His slumber.
He stopped Anders, and He must pay for that.
Anders will make sure He regrets letting him live.
Anders will wander through biting cold, warmed by nothing but his burning hatred.
Walk through the fires of Hell, cooled by nothing but his ice-cold heart.
Anders will force It to their knees, claim It's power as his own, and use it to battle Him.
Anders will get his revenge, armoured from head to toe, armed with the firepower to put the worlds strongest armies to shame,
or armed with nothing but his legs, and protected only by his burning wrath.
It does not matter. Anders Tale's journey can only end toe to toe against Him.
