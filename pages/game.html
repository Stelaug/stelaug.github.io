How to install Anders Tale 2:
1. Press to open the google drive install folder.
2. Press the install button () in the upper right corner.
3. Find the .zip file in your downloads folder. Right click it and press "Extract All...".
4. Once the files have been extracted, right click the anderstail.exe file and press "Open".
5. Now you're good to go! You can either play our story mode in singleplayer or play with friends in our multiplayer gamemodes.
